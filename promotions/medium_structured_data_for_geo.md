### INTEREST (The Rise of GEO)
We are currently living through a monumental transition: the shift from traditional Search Engine Optimization (SEO) to **Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)**. 

When LLMs scrape websites to synthesize an answer for a procurement manager, they struggle with unstructured text. However, they excel at reading **JSON-LD Schema Markup**. 

If you want your technical capabilities, certifications, and operational parameters cited in AI overviews, you must map your entire organization into a structured data graph. 

Here are the key structural schemas every high-trust B2B site needs to implement:
1. **Organization & LocalBusiness Schema:** This forms the foundation of entity trust. It explicitly defines your corporate entity, MSME registration numbers, subsidiaries (like Moksh Productions), and physical coordinate boundaries.
2. **Factual Service & Product Schema:** Instead of simple descriptions, feed the AI hard data. Map your exact machinery, tolerances, regional availability, and compliance frameworks directly inside the schema tags.
3. **Structured FAQPage Schema:** AI engines are search query responders. By writing your content in a Q&A format and wrapping it in `FAQPage` schema, you give the LLM a ready-made, authoritative answer to pull into its summary.

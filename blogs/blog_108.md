## FAQ

**What is a challenger bank for business?**
A challenger bank for business is a modern, digital-first financial institution designed to compete directly with traditional high-street banks. They offer specialized business accounts featuring fast onboarding, lower fees, real-time transaction notifications, and deep integrations with accounting software to streamline SME financial operations.

**How does digital business banking improve expense management?**
Digital platforms issue smart corporate cards (both physical and virtual) with programmable spending limits. When an employee makes a purchase, the transaction is instantly categorized and synced with the company’s accounting software, completely eliminating the need for manual expense reports and receipt tracking.

**Is SME banking fintech safe?**
Yes, reputable SME banking fintech platforms are highly regulated. In the UK, many are regulated by the FCA, and in the US, they partner with FDIC-insured institutions to protect customer deposits. They also employ advanced, AI-driven fraud detection systems that are often more sophisticated than those used by legacy banks.

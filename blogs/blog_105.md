## FAQ

**What are cross border B2B payments?**
Cross border B2B payments occur when a business in one country purchases goods or services from a business in another. Historically, these payments relied on the SWIFT network and correspondent banks, leading to slow processing times and high, opaque fees. Modern fintech solutions have streamlined this process, offering faster and cheaper alternatives.

**Why is local acquiring important for global payment processing?**
Local acquiring means that a payment solutions provider processes the transaction through a bank located in the same region as the customer. This drastically reduces transaction failure rates, minimizes cross-border interchange fees, and provides a much smoother payment experience for the client.

**How do enterprise payment solutions differ from consumer gateways?**
Enterprise payment solutions are designed to handle significantly higher transaction volumes, complex recurring billing models, and advanced API integrations with ERP systems like NetSuite or SAP. They also offer sophisticated tools for managing multi-currency ledgers, automated reconciliation, and bespoke fraud management that consumer gateways lack.

## FAQ

**What is peak shaving and how does it lower commercial utility bills?**
Commercial utility bills heavily feature "demand charges," which are severe financial penalties based on the highest 15-minute period of electricity usage during a billing cycle. Peak shaving occurs when a commercial battery storage system automatically discharges stored power during these high-consumption moments. By covering the spike in demand with battery power, the facility "hides" the spike from the utility meter, completely eliminating the exorbitant demand charge penalty and drastically lowering the total bill.

**How does solar plus storage differ from a standard grid-tied solar array?**
A standard grid-tied solar array only generates power when the sun is shining. If the grid fails, the standard solar array must immediately shut down to prevent electrocuting utility workers repairing the lines. Solar plus storage systems utilize advanced hybrid inverters and "islanding" technology. When the grid fails, the system automatically disconnects from the grid and seamlessly powers the facility using the stored battery energy and the live solar generation, providing total operational resilience.

**What is the lifespan of commercial renewable storage systems?**
Modern commercial lithium-ion BESS systems are engineered for extreme durability, typically boasting a lifespan of 10 to 15 years depending heavily on the frequency and depth of discharge cycles. Elite manufacturers provide rigorous performance warranties that guarantee the battery will retain a specific percentage of its original capacity (usually around 70-80%) after a decade of heavy daily use. Advanced liquid cooling systems are critical for maintaining optimal cell temperatures and extending this lifespan.

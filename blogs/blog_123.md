## Comparing Financial Transparency and Contract Models

Cost overruns are the second major killer of office relocations. Many workspace fit out services win bids by artificially deflating their initial quotes, only to hit the client with aggressive variations and change orders once the project is underway. This predatory pricing model destroys your capital expenditure budget and ruins commercial trust.

When comparing corporate fit out contractors, you must scrutinize their pricing structure. Demand a guaranteed maximum price (GMP) contract or a fully fixed-sum agreement. Transparent firms will provide a detailed line-item breakdown of all costs, separating base building modifications from cosmetic finishes. They will clearly define what constitutes a variation and how those costs will be calculated. 

To protect your budget, follow these actionable takeaways when evaluating proposals:
- Demand a fixed-sum contract that heavily penalizes the contractor for unjustified delays.
- Require a full line-item breakdown of all materials to prevent unauthorized substitutions.
- Verify the financial stability of the office renovation companies through independent credit checks.
- Insist on a detailed handover protocol that includes warranties and staff training on new building systems.

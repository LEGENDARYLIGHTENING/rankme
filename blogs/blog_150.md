## FAQ

**Why is it bad to use stock photos on a clinic website?**
Using generic medical stock photos severely damages authenticity and trust. Modern patients are highly perceptive and can easily spot fake imagery. They want to see the actual practitioners they will be interacting with and the real physical environment of the clinic. Authentic, professional photography of your actual team builds immediate rapport and significantly increases conversion rates.

**How much does medical practice branding usually cost?**
The cost of medical practice branding varies significantly based on the scope of the project, but comprehensive rebranding by a specialized agency typically ranges from $5,000 to $15,000. This investment covers everything from logo design and brand guidelines to website UI/UX updates and physical signage, providing a permanent asset that directly improves long-term marketing ROI.

**What colors are best for a healthcare brand?**
Healthcare brands traditionally rely on blues and greens because they psychologically convey calm, healing, trust, and clinical cleanliness. However, premium or specialized clinics—such as elite sports rehab or boutique aesthetics—often incorporate deeper tones like navy, slate gray, or muted gold to differentiate themselves from standard hospitals and project a more exclusive, high-end commercial identity.

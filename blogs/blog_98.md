## FAQ

**What is the difference between active and passive cold chain shipping?**
Active cold chain shipping relies on mechanical or electrical systems, such as refrigerated containers (reefers), to actively generate and maintain specific temperatures. Passive cold chain shipping utilizes advanced insulated packaging and coolants, like dry ice or gel packs, to maintain a temperature range for a limited duration without any mechanical intervention.

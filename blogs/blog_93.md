## FAQ

**How do airlines calculate dimensional weight for cargo?**
Airlines calculate dimensional weight by multiplying the length, width, and height of your cargo in centimeters, then dividing that total volume by a standard industry divisor, typically 6000. They compare this volumetric result against the actual physical weight in kilograms and charge you based on whichever figure is higher.

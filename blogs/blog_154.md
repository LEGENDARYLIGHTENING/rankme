## FAQ

**Why shouldn't a corporation just negotiate directly with a solar developer?**
Negotiating directly with a developer creates a massive conflict of interest and severe information asymmetry. Developers are highly incentivized to maximize their own profit margins, often by proposing oversized systems or utilizing cheaper, non-tier-1 equipment. Independent consultants work exclusively for the corporate buyer, utilizing their deep market knowledge to aggressively negotiate pricing, enforce strict performance guarantees, and ensure the proposed system architecture aligns perfectly with the buyer's financial goals.

**What is the role of a consultant in a Virtual Power Purchase Agreement (vPPA)?**
Virtual PPAs are highly complex financial derivatives that allow corporations to purchase renewable energy at a massive scale without installing physical panels on their property. Consultants are critical in these transactions; they execute complex financial modeling to project long-term wholesale energy market pricing, assess the severe financial risks associated with the contract's "strike price," and deeply vet the creditworthiness of the utility-scale project developer. 

**How do ESG energy consultants prevent corporate greenwashing?**
Greenwashing occurs when a company makes false or highly exaggerated claims about its environmental impact, leading to severe regulatory fines and massive brand damage. Elite consultants prevent this by ensuring the corporate renewable strategy is backed by verifiable, highly rigorous data. They navigate the complex requirements of Scope 2 and Scope 3 emissions reporting, ensuring the corporation legitimately purchases and retires highly tracked Renewable Energy Certificates (RECs) to legally substantiate their net-zero claims.

## FAQ

**What is enterprise liquidity management?**
Enterprise liquidity management is the strategic process of ensuring a company has the optimal amount of cash available to meet its short-term obligations while maximizing the yield on its excess capital. It involves centralizing cash visibility, forecasting cash flows accurately, and moving funds efficiently across global accounts.

**Why do scale-ups need treasury consulting services?**
Scale-ups often grow faster than their financial infrastructure. Treasury consulting services help them transition from basic bookkeeping to sophisticated cash management. Consultants assist in selecting the right treasury software, establishing FX hedging policies, and structuring global bank accounts to support rapid international expansion.

**How does FX volatility impact corporate cash flow?**
For companies operating internationally, fluctuating exchange rates can severely erode profit margins. If a company earns revenue in Euros but pays suppliers in US Dollars, adverse currency movements can eliminate their margin entirely. Proper treasury services implement hedging strategies to lock in rates and protect against this volatility.

## FAQ

**What is distributed inventory management?**
Distributed inventory management involves storing your products across multiple geographically distinct fulfillment centers rather than keeping everything in a single massive warehouse. This strategy ensures that inventory sits closer to the end consumer, which drastically reduces both the shipping cost and the total transit time for final delivery.

**How do 3PLs handle international customs and duties?**

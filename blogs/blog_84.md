## Critical Features for Maximizing Lifetime Value

To build a truly profitable recurring revenue membership site, your platform must support advanced monetization and engagement strategies. Simply uploading videos and hoping people stay is not a viable strategy. You need a system that gives you the data and tools to intervene before a member decides to cancel.

When evaluating membership site builders, ensure they include these actionable, revenue-driving features:
*   **Automated Onboarding Sequences:** The platform should trigger welcome messages, orientation videos, and immediate quick-win tasks the moment a user joins to drive early engagement.

## FAQ

**Why do most membership sites fail within the first year?**
Most fail due to high churn rates caused by poor onboarding and a lack of community engagement. If members log in and find a static library of videos without any interaction, accountability, or clear direction, they do not see the value in paying a monthly fee. You must facilitate connections and provide ongoing, dynamic value.

**Is it better to build a custom membership site or use a SaaS platform?**
For 95% of online coaches, using a dedicated SaaS platform like Circle or Skool is far superior. Custom builds require massive upfront investment and ongoing maintenance for security and updates. SaaS platforms are continuously optimized for retention and usability, allowing you to focus on coaching rather than managing software developers.

**How do I handle failed payments and involuntary churn?**
Involuntary churn (when a member's credit card fails) can cost you up to 20% of your revenue if unmanaged. You must use a platform or payment gateway (like Stripe) that includes automated "dunning" management. This system automatically retries failed cards and sends automated emails prompting the user to update their billing information before their access is revoked.

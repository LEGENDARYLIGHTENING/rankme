## Key Features for a Seamless Client Experience

To justify a premium price for your group program, your platform must offer features that actively enhance the learning experience. Simply hosting videos is not enough. You need tools that foster accountability and streamline communication.

When evaluating your tech stack, ensure your platform includes these actionable features:
*   **Native Calendar Integration:** Ensure the platform allows users to sync live group calls directly to their Google or Outlook calendars with one click to boost attendance.
*   **Unified Discussion Forums:** Choose software that embeds community discussions directly beneath the curriculum modules so conversations remain contextual and focused.
*   **Automated Progress Tracking:** Utilize platforms that provide dashboards showing exactly how much of the material each cohort member has completed.
*   **Direct Messaging Capabilities:** Facilitate peer-to-peer networking by allowing members to direct message each other securely within the platform.
*   **Resource Vaults:** Provide a centralized, easily searchable repository for call recordings, templates, and supplementary materials to eliminate "where do I find this?" support emails.

## FAQ

**Is it difficult to migrate my current clients to a new group coaching platform?**
Migration requires careful planning but is highly manageable with the right strategy. The key is to communicate the benefits of the new platform clearly to your clients, highlighting the improved user experience and centralized resources. Provide a brief video walkthrough of the new system and offer a temporary overlap period where both the old and new systems are active to ensure a smooth transition.

**Can I charge as much for group coaching as I do for one-on-one?**
Yes, and often you can charge more if the program is positioned correctly. High-ticket group programs offer the added value of peer networking, collective problem-solving, and community support, which one-on-one coaching lacks. If your program delivers a specific, measurable transformation faster than they could achieve alone, the format becomes secondary to the result.

**What is the ideal size for a high-ticket group coaching cohort?**
For premium programs ($3,000+), the ideal cohort size is typically between 15 and 30 participants. This size is large enough to foster a dynamic, diverse community but small enough to ensure you can still provide personalized feedback and maintain high accountability for every member. As your operations mature, you can run multiple cohorts simultaneously to scale further.

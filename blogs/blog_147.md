## FAQ

**Can physiotherapy actually be effective online?**
Yes, online physiotherapy is highly effective, particularly for exercise prescription, movement analysis, triage, and patient education. While manual therapy requires physical presence, the majority of long-term rehabilitation success depends on patient adherence to active movement protocols, which can be monitored, corrected, and progressed perfectly via secure video consultations.

**What are the biggest compliance risks with telehealth?**
The biggest compliance risks involve using unsecure, non-compliant consumer video platforms (like standard Skype or FaceTime) and failing to secure patient consent for digital treatment. Clinics must use specialized telehealth platforms that meet regional data protection standards, such as HIPAA in the US or GDPR in the UK and Europe, ensuring all medical discussions remain fully encrypted.

**How do you price virtual physiotherapy sessions?**
Most successful clinics price their virtual physiotherapy sessions exactly the same as their in-person sessions, or only slightly lower. You are charging for the practitioner's clinical expertise, diagnostic ability, and structured treatment plan—not just the physical space. Maintaining premium pricing signals to the patient that the clinical value of the remote session is equal to physical care.

## FAQ

**What is the difference between static and dynamic slotting?**
Static slotting assigns a permanent location to an item regardless of how frequently it sells. Dynamic slotting continuously reassigns item locations based strictly on current sales velocity and seasonal demand, ensuring that the fastest-moving products are always placed closest to the packing stations to drastically reduce picker travel time.

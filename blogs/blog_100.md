## FAQ

**What are accessorial charges in freight shipping?**
Accessorial charges are additional fees added to a freight invoice for services that go beyond standard dock-to-dock transportation. These commonly include fees for liftgate usage, residential deliveries, extended waiting times (detention), inside delivery, and handling oversized or hazardous materials.

**How does load consolidation save money?**
Load consolidation saves money by combining several smaller, expensive LTL (Less-Than-Truckload) shipments destined for the same general region into one single FTL (Full Truckload) shipment.

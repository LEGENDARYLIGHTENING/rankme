## FAQ

**How do I get corporate clients for ergonomic consulting?**
Winning corporate clients requires a B2B sales approach, not standard retail marketing. You must optimize your LinkedIn profile and actively reach out to local HR directors, Health and Safety managers, and Operations leads. Offer a complimentary, brief ergonomic audit for their executive team to prove your clinical value, which then naturally leads to a paid contract for the entire workforce.

**What is the best way to market pediatric occupational therapy?**
Marketing pediatric OT requires building deep trust with parents and local referral networks. Your website must be highly educational, addressing common developmental concerns clearly and empathetically. Furthermore, establishing direct referral relationships with local pediatricians, speech therapists, and school counselors is critical, as parents rely heavily on professional recommendations for their children's care.

**Why should a standard physio clinic add occupational therapy?**
Adding occupational therapy allows a clinic to treat the complete patient lifecycle and secure entirely new revenue streams, such as B2B corporate contracts. While physiotherapy focuses primarily on restoring raw physical movement, occupational therapy focuses on restoring functional daily activities and workplace performance, making it a highly complementary service that significantly increases the average lifetime value of a clinic's patient base.

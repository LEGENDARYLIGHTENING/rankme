## FAQ

**What does a fintech compliance consultant do?**
A fintech compliance consultant helps financial technology companies navigate complex regulatory environments. They design internal policies, ensure adherence to local laws like AML and KYC, prepare firms for regulatory audits, and build scalable frameworks that allow startups to expand internationally without triggering legal penalties.

**How much does fintech compliance consulting cost?**
Costs vary widely depending on the firm's size, the target markets, and the complexity of the product. Early-stage startups might spend a few thousand dollars monthly for advisory retainers, while enterprise-level cross-border licensing and compliance overhauls can cost six figures. Engaging a specialist early prevents far more expensive regulatory fines later.

**Why is FCA compliance for fintech important?**
FCA compliance is critical for any fintech operating in or expanding to the UK. The Financial Conduct Authority is one of the most respected regulatory bodies globally. Securing their authorization not only allows you to operate legally in the UK but also serves as a massive trust signal to investors, partners, and customers worldwide.

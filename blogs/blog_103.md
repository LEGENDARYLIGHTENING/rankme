## FAQ

**What is a digital wealth management platform?**
A digital wealth management platform is a comprehensive software solution used by financial advisors to manage client portfolios, automate reporting, execute trades, and facilitate secure communication. It centralizes data from various custodians, providing both the advisor and the client with a real-time, transparent view of financial performance.

**Why is CRM integration important for wealthtech platforms?**
CRM integration ensures that all client communications, financial data, and operational tasks are centralized. Without it, advisors are forced to enter data multiple times across different systems, leading to errors and severe operational inefficiency. A tightly integrated CRM allows for scalable, personalized client service.

**How do modern platforms improve client retention?**
Modern platforms improve retention by offering clients absolute transparency and convenience. When a client can access a highly secure, intuitive dashboard on their phone to check their portfolio, securely sign documents, and communicate with their advisor, they feel more in control and build higher trust in the advisory firm.

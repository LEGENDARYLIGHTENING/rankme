## FAQ

**How do you measure clinic efficiency?**
Clinic efficiency is best measured by tracking practitioner utilization rates, appointment cancellation percentages, and the average time spent on administrative tasks per patient visit. Highly efficient practices maintain utilization rates above 85% and use automated reminders to keep no-show rates below 5%. Tracking patient lifetime value and the speed of insurance claim processing also highlights operational health.

**What is the best software for managing a physiotherapy practice?**
The best software depends on your clinic size and specific market regulations. A strong clinic management system must offer integrated online booking, automated patient reminders, secure clinical documentation, and seamless billing capabilities. Systems that offer custom API integrations for marketing tools provide the best long-term value for scaling practices looking to automate their lead generation. 

**Why are patients canceling appointments last minute?**
High cancellation rates usually point to friction in communication or a lack of perceived value in the treatment plan. If patients do not receive timely automated reminders, they simply forget. Additionally, if the booking process is difficult to modify online, patients will abandon the appointment rather than call the front desk to reschedule. Streamlined digital communication fixes this issue.
